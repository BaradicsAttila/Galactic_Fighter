Objective:

- Pilot your spaceship, destroy enemy ships, and avoid incoming bullets. Survive as long as possible.

Controls:

- Move: W/A/S/D

- Shoot: Spacebar

How to Play:

- Navigate your ship to dodge enemy buletts

- Press Spacebar to fire your weapons and take down enemies.

- Enemies appear every few seconds.

- Avoid getting hit, your ship can only take three damage.

Game Over:

- The Game ends when you lose all your life and your ship gets destroyed

Tip:

- Keep moving. Standing still makes you an easy target!
